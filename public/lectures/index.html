Speech and Workshop about Chatbots in Xanthi

March 2018

In Xanthi, I conducted in cooperation with EESTEC LC Xanthi, a two-day workshop about chatbots to 12 software engineering students. Before the workshop, I delivered a speech about the state of the chatbot technology nowadays and what is happening in Greece around it.
